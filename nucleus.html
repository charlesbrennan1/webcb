Nucleus Research

I joined Nucleus Research in January 2023 as a Senior Research Analyst, diving into the world of enterprise technology research and analysis. My focus centers on ERP, supply chain management, and operational technology solutions, where I evaluate how these systems drive business value for organizations across industries. From day one, I've been responsible for producing in-depth research reports, conducting vendor briefings, and analyzing market trends that help both technology buyers and vendors make informed decisions. My work involves everything from ROI case studies and technology assessments to competitive analysis and market positioning research.

What distinguishes my approach at Nucleus is the combination of hands-on operational experience with analytical rigor. Having worked directly in supply chain operations at TLF Apparel, I bring a practitioner's perspective to technology evaluation that resonates with both end users and solution providers. I regularly participate in industry conferences, conduct webinars, and engage in speaking opportunities to share insights on digital transformation trends. My research has covered everything from warehouse management systems and inventory optimization to broader ERP modernization strategies, always with a focus on quantifiable business outcomes.

The work showcased below represents the breadth of my contributions to the Nucleus Research practice: comprehensive research reports, thought leadership content, webinar presentations, podcast appearances, and speaking engagements. Each piece reflects my commitment to delivering actionable insights that help organizations navigate their technology decisions with confidence, backed by data-driven analysis and real-world operational understanding.
